About

Task automation with Python simplifies and streamlines daily tasks, enhancing efficiency and productivity by reducing manual effort and saving time.

About my project

My project focuses on automating the retrieval of daily US news using Python coding. Given the multitude of news channels in the US, it's impractical to extract every news item, so I've developed a task automation system that can efficiently extract the top US news stories. This solution helps individuals avoid unnecessary distractions from less relevant notifications, ensuring that they receive only the news they desire.
